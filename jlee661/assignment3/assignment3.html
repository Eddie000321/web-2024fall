Tesla Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is designed to offer high performance, efficiency, and safety. It is available in different variants, providing flexibility for every driver. Experience the thrill of driving with incredible speed and the comfort of long range options.

  • Range: Up to 584 km (EPA est.)
  • Top Speed: Up to 262 km/h
  • Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in as little as 3.1 seconds
  • Full Self Driving available as an option ($11,000)
